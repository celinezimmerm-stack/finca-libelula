Where are we located?

We are located in the San Blas Valley, 30 minutes from the town of San Carlos, Antioquia. We are part of an ecological corridor of more than 800 hectares made up of private nature reserves working around three fundamental pillars: conservation, food sovereignty, and integration with the local community.

Our project

Preservation

We protect biodiversity through our Civil Society Nature Reserve. We develop internship and research programs with Colombian and international universities, and we are working toward the creation of a habitat bank in partnership with the NGO Change the World and the Darién Foundation.

Agroecological Production

An integrated farm operating in a closed-loop system. Production of organic cheese, high-quality pork, and black tilapia. Cultivation and processing of cacao and sacha inchi, our flagship products.

Sustainable Territories

We build sustainable territories through collective work, integrating local communities and strengthening the region through community workdays and collaborative initiatives.
